The OZeWAI Conference 2018 schedule is now live!

There's only 6 weeks until the OZeWAI conference kicks off. We have an amazing line-up of presenters spread over three full days including keynotes from Nic Steenhout and Annie Parker.

There are over 25 other presentations, covering topics such as WCAG 2.1, CAPTCHA, Comprehensive Testing, Content Accessibility, Inclusive Publishing, Accessible Design Systems, Artificial Intelligence and much more.

We also have an update on International Association of Accessibility Professionals and how it relates to the W3C.

And finally, we have two fantastic workshops. The first is a deeep dive into WCAG 2.1. The second explores Accessibility, inclusion and human rights in Australia: a workshop for experts to share and learn.
